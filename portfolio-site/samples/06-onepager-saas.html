The Problem

Your CI/CD pipeline is a black box

Engineering teams are shipping faster than ever — and flying blind when things go wrong. The result:

  • Flaky tests waste hours before anyone figures out they're not real failures
  • Deployment failures surface in production, not in the pipeline
  • Post-incident debugging takes hours because nobody knows which change caused what
  • Engineers skip tests or delay deploys because they don't trust the pipeline

How It Works

Intelligent observability across your entire delivery pipeline

  • 🔍

    AI Root Cause Analysis

    When a build fails, DevFlow identifies the likely cause — changed dependency, test environment issue, or specific commit — in seconds, not hours.

  • 🧪

    Flaky Test Detection

    Automatically identifies tests that fail non-deterministically. Quarantines them to a separate suite so they stop blocking real deployments.

  • 🚀

    Deployment Risk Scoring

    Before you deploy, DevFlow scores the risk based on change size, test coverage gaps, and historical failure patterns for that service.

  • 📊

    DORA Metrics Dashboard

    Deployment frequency, lead time, change failure rate, and MTTR — automatically tracked, no instrumentation required.
