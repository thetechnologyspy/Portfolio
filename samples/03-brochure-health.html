The Challenge

Clinicians are making critical decisions without the information they need

The average clinician sees 20–30 patients per shift, documents for 2–3 hours, and has access to a treatment evidence base that doubles every 73 days. No human can keep up. The consequences are real.

  • Diagnostic errors affect an estimated 12 million US patients annually
  • Clinicians spend 34–55% of their time on EHR documentation instead of patient care
  • Care variation across facilities costs health systems millions in avoidable readmissions
  • Burnout from administrative burden is driving record turnover in clinical staff
"We have more clinical evidence available than ever before. And less time to apply it than ever before. That gap is where patients are getting hurt."
— Chief Medical Officer, Regional Health System (500+ beds)

The Solution

Clinical intelligence embedded where decisions happen

MediMind AI integrates directly into your existing EHR workflow. Clinicians see AI-generated insights, differential diagnoses, and evidence summaries in the same interface they already use — no new login, no context switch, no workflow disruption.

🔬

Differential Diagnosis Support

Surfaces ranked differentials based on symptoms, labs, history, and current literature — highlighting red flags the clinician may not have considered.

📋

Ambient Documentation

AI listens to the patient encounter and generates draft notes, referral letters, and discharge summaries. Clinicians review and approve — not type from scratch.

💊

Drug Interaction Alerts

Real-time checking against the patient's full medication list, renal function, allergies, and contraindications — with severity-weighted alert prioritisation to reduce alert fatigue.

📊

Protocol Adherence Monitoring

Automatically checks treatment decisions against your institution's clinical pathways and flags deviations — without interrupting the care workflow.

🔄

EHR-Native Integration

Compatible with Epic, Cerner, Oracle Health, and 50+ other systems via HL7 FHIR APIs. Average implementation time: 8 weeks.

🔐

On-Premise Deployment

Patient data never leaves your infrastructure. MediMind AI runs inside your environment, fully air-gapped if required, with your security team in control.
